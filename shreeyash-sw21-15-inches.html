Modes

Shreeyash SW 21 is a dedicated Neonatal & Pediatric Ventilator provided with different modes of ventilation to assist the patient as per the need.

It is a pressure & volume controlled, time cycled, continuous flow respirator that is intended for Neonatal & Pediatric use.

Equipped with High Frequency (Flow Chopping), Auto mode facility and non-invasive High Flow Oxygen therapy put this device in the class of most updated ventilators.

  • 15 inches large touch screen is provided for viewing the graphics with Rotary Knob for setting the parameters.
  • The Screen displays three scalar graphs of Pressure, Flow and Volume.
  • Three loops are also displayed: Pressure- Flow, Flow- volume and Pressure Volume.
  • Shreeyash SW 21 is provided with HFFC (High Frequency Flow Chopping Mechanism) mode for treating LBW babies. In this mode: The clinician can set the Frequency from 5 to 15 Hertzs, I:E ratio can be set from 1:1 & 1:2, and the power (Amplitude) can be set from 0 to 100%. MAP can be set up to 30 CM H2O, Bias flow is servo controlled display of delivered Tidal Volume & DCO2 helps the clinician to non Invasively monitor the pulmonary mechanics.
  • Auto mode facility provided in ACMV mode.
  • Auto Mode and backup ventilation provided in CPAP + PS Mode.
  • Non-invasive High Flow Nasal CPAP (HFNC Mode).
  • On line Inspiratory & Expiratory Hold.
  • Provision to connect Nebulizer kit & ETCO2 sensor.
  • Inbuilt Suction facility provided.
  • 2 hours battery backup.
