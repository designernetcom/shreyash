Shreeyash 900 DV, A Simple & Reliable Ventilator

In 2019, Shreeyash Electro Medicals expanded its expertise into adult ventilator manufacturing by introducing the Shreeyash 900 DV Adult Ventilator. Building on over 16 years of experience in the neonatal ventilator space, the company ventured into this critical area in response to growing healthcare needs.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, the demand for reliable, Made-in-India ventilators surged, and

Shreeyash was approached by higher authorities to develop a solution that could support the country's healthcare system. After months of deep research and the use of cutting-edge technology, the Shreeyash 900 DV was developed, designed to deliver high performance and reliability in intensive care situations.

Before its market release, the ventilator underwent a rigorous six-month testing phase, involving a team of 30 leading intensivists across several renowned hospitals in India. Their feedback and performance evaluations helped fine-tune the machine, ensuring it met the highest standards of care.

Following its outstanding performance in clinical settings, the Shreeyash 900 DV was recognized as the "Best Ventilator in India" and was awarded a grant of ₹43.5 lakhs, solidifying Shreeyash's position as a trusted leader in the medical equipment industry.

Introduction of Shreeyash 900 Ventilator:

Shreeyash SW 21 is a dedicated Adult & Pediatric Ventilator provided with different modes of ventilation to assist the patient as per the need.

Shreeyash 900 DV is a Volume & Pressure controlled ventilator with advanced modes of Invasive & Non-Invasive Ventilation to treat patients suffering from various lung diseases.

The PRVC. MMV. APRV, CPAP Plus PS mode & the HFNC mode puts in the category of the most updated ventilators.

Display :
  • 15 inches large touch screen is provided for viewing the graphics with Rotary Knob for setting the parameters.
  • The Screen displays three scalar graphs of Pressure, Flow, and Volume.
  • Three loops are also displayed: Pressure-Flow, Flow-Volume, and Pressure-Volume.
  • The Auto Mode provided in ACMV and CPAP + PS modes on this ventilator is highly beneficial for clinicians and attending staff, ensuring safe and effective ventilation for the patient.
  • CPAP plus pressure support. This specialty mode puts the ventilator in the category of the most updated ventilators & is a boon for patients with "FLAIL" Chest.
  • The setting of background ventilation in CPAP mode ensures effective ventilation of the patient in case of Apnea.
