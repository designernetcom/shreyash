About Us
Shreeyash Electro Medicals

Shreeyash Electro Medicals has been helping to save thousands of lives across Pan India for the last 34 years using our Life-Saving Equipment. We have expertise in Manufacturing and Exporting a range of ventilation products that are made available in a varied range of specifications, to meet the diversified requirements of our valuable customers. We are extremely conscious of the quality of the offered assembly and this is the reason, we use premium quality raw materials for the manufacturing operation. At our company, we have established a state-of-the-art infrastructure facility that is equipped with basic amenities and machines that are based on modern & advanced technology in the market. Moreover, we have recruited a team of experienced and skilled professionals, who are well trained to understand the varied requirements of our reputed customers.

"It is a great idea that gives birth to an innovation, not necessarily higher educational degrees," says the Director Mr. Sudhir Waghmare, he has been able to establish a stand-alone position in the market. His rich industry experience, continuous motivation, and sound business understanding have enabled our organization to gain phenomenal success in this competitive market.

The manufacturing activity is carried out in Pune in ISO 13485 certified premises. We have been manufacturing newborn life-saving devices, including Bubble CPAP, Neonatal Ventilators, and High-Frequency Oscillatory Ventilators, since 2004. With this strong background of clinical & technical expertise, Shreeyash has Ventured into Manufacturing various ground breaking devices.
