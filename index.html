Our Human Milk Bank Equipments

A Human Milk Bank is a facility dedicated to the safe collection, processing, and distribution of donated human breast milk. This milk is often given to premature or critically ill infants whose mothers cannot provide enough milk.

our innovation
Pioneering Medical Device Innovations

At Shreeyash Electro Medicals, innovation is at the heart of everything we do. For over three decades, we’ve been dedicated to developing pioneering medical devices that set new standards in neonatal and adult care. Our commitment to advancing technology is driven by a singular goal: to enhance patient outcomes and support healthcare professionals with state-of-the-art solutions.

read more
1
1. Bubble CPAP (2003)

As the first company in India to manufacture Bubble CPAP, we revolutionized neonatal respiratory support. This non-invasive system delivers continuous positive airway pressure to aid breathing in newborns, providing a critical alternative to more invasive treatments.

2
2. Neonatal Ventilator (2004)

Our Neonatal Ventilator, introduced in 2004, represents a significant advancement in respiratory care for premature and critically ill infants. Designed with precision and flexibility, it offers various ventilation modes to address the diverse needs of neonates.

3
3. High-Frequency Oscillatory Ventilator (2006)

In 2006, we introduced the High-Frequency Oscillatory Ventilator (HFOV), enhancing our ability to manage severe respiratory conditions. HFOV provides rapid, small breaths to improve lung function while minimizing the risk of lung injury.

4
4. Conductive Patient Warming Blankets (2013)

Ensuring optimal body temperature is essential for newborns in critical care. Our Conductive Patient Warming Blankets, developed in 2013, offer controlled and consistent warming, supporting recovery and stability during medical procedures.

5
5. Fully Automatic Human Milk Pasteurizer (2018)

In 2018, we launched the Fully Automatic Human Milk Pasteurizer, designed to ensure the safe pasteurization of human milk. This innovation preserves the milk’s nutritional value while protecting against harmful bacteria, supporting the health and development of newborns.

6
6. Shreeyash 900 Adult Ventilator (2020)

The Shreeyash 900 Adult Ventilator, reflects our commitment to expanding our expertise into adult care. Launched in 2020, this ventilator features an Auto Mode for adaptive and precise ventilation. Clinically validated and certified for safety, it meets global standards for critical care. It was awarded the Best Ventilator in India and showcased our exemplary vision willingness to adapt to the current needs.

7
7. Bilicare Focus Patented Phototherapy System – 2021

In 2021, we introduced the Bilicare Focus, a patented phototherapy system designed to provide effective treatment for neonatal jaundice. This innovative system combines advanced LED technology with precise wavelength control, ensuring optimal therapeutic outcomes.
