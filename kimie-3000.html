Kimie 3000ml

Overview
Special Functions
Modes

Kimie is a fully automatic pasteurizer designed for precision and reliability, controlled by intelligent microcontrollers. It features both an inbuilt heating and cooling system, with a digital display showing the milk and water temperatures. The system is eco-friendly, using only 15 liters of water in a closed-loop process, allowing water reuse. It operates without the need for special plumbing connections and is compact and portable, mounted on 4 castors (2 with locking devices). The unit accommodates up to 14 milk containers and includes an automatic drain facility for hot and cold water after each cycle. Kimie follows the Baum/Holder Method, ensuring scientific precision with automatic heating and rapid cooling in one chamber.

  • Power Requirements: 230 Volts AC +/-10% 50 Hz / 110 Volts 60 Hz.
  • Automation: Fully automatic with intelligent microcontroller and servo control.
  • Display: Three-digit digital display for milk temperature, elapsed time, and water temperature.
  • Milk Sensor: Special sensor ensures accuracy of milk temperature.
  • Water Sensor: Separate sensor controls bath temperature.
  • Heating & Cooling System: Heats milk to 62.5°C +/- 0.5°C. Holds milk at 62.5°C for 30 minutes. Cools milk to 25°C in 5 minutes, 10°C in 10 minutes, and 4°C within 30 minutes.
  • Capacity: Accommodates 14 milk containers of 250 ml each.
  • Eco-friendly: Uses 15 liters of water, which can be reused for the next cycle.
  • Safety Features: Water overflow facility and auto drain for hot and cold water after each cycle.
  • Compliance: Follows Baum/Holder Method for pasteurization.
  • Mobility: Mounted on 4 castors, 2 with locks.
  • Alarms: Inbuilt alarms to monitor the pasteurization process.
