Milk Thawing Machine

The Fully Automatic Milk Thawing Machine is a state-of-the-art solution for warming frozen milk efficiently. It incorporates a water pump and heater for uniform temperature control, guided by an intelligent microcontroller that ensures the desired temperature is maintained. The machine can thaw up to 1500 ml of milk in a single cycle and includes various safety features such as low water level detection and automatic shut-off if temperatures exceed 38.5°C. The system is designed with user convenience in mind, featuring an LED display, transparent door, and stainless steel cage for easy monitoring and handling during the thawing process.

Overview
Specifications
Features

The Fully Automatic Milk Thawing Machine is a highly efficient system designed to thaw frozen milk quickly and safely. It features an intelligent microcontroller-based system that ensures precise temperature control, warming milk from -18°C to 37°C in just 30 minutes. The machine uses a forced hot water warming system, circulating water uniformly to avoid any hot or cold spots. With a capacity to thaw up to 1500 ml of milk in one cycle, it is perfect for clinical and laboratory settings. Safety is a top priority, with features like automatic shut-off in case of overheating and water level sensors to prevent operation during low water levels.

  • Comprises of a water pump and heater for uniform warm water circulation in the cabinet.
  • Incorporates a forced Hot Water Warming system for efficient milk thawing.
  • Warms milk from -18°C to 37°C in 30 minutes (depending on room temperature).
  • Intelligent Microcontroller maintains the temperature as set by the clinician.
  • Special platinum PT-100 Sensor with steel capsule ensures accurate bath water temperature control.
  • 3-digit LED digital display shows the set and actual water temperature inside the chamber.
  • Accommodates six 250 ml stainless steel milk containers (1500 ml total capacity per cycle).
  • Stainless Steel cage holds the milk containers securely during the process.
  • Front door with transparent acrylic panel allows easy access and visibility of the process.
  • Closed-loop system circulates hot water to ensure uniform warming.
  • Illumination lamp provided for observation during the thawing process.
  • Mounted on a trolley with four castors, two equipped with locking devices for mobility and stability.
  • Servo-controlled system with a maximum temperature setting of 38.5°C.
  • LED display shows actual and set temperature for precise control.
  • Feather touch keys for easy temperature setting adjustments.
  • Platinum PT-100 probe with stainless steel encapsulation for accurate temperature measurement.
  • LED indications for cycle status, temperature, and water level warnings.
  • Water level sensor prevents operation during low water levels for safety.
  • Automatic safety shut-off if water temperature exceeds 38.5°C.
  • Electrical Rating: 230 VAC ± 10%, 50 Hz, with maximum wattage of 750 watts.
  • Best operating environment: 22°C to 28°C.
